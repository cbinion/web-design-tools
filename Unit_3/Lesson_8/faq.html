When was Jon Stewart born?
He was born on November 28th 1962 at Doctors Hospital in New York City's Upper East Side to Marian (née Laskin), a teacher and later educational consultant, and Donald Leibowitz (1931-2013), an energy coordinator for the New Jersey Department of the Treasury. Stewart's family members are Ashkenazi Jewish immigrants to America from Poland, Ukraine, and Belarus. One of his grandfathers was born in Manzhouli (now part of China).

What is Jon Stewart's birth name?
He was born Jonathan Stuart Leibowitz. He began using the stage name Jon Stewart by dropping his last name and changing the spelling of his middle name, Stuart, to Stewart. He often jokes that it was because people had trouble pronouncing Leibowitz, or it "sounded too Hollywood" (a reference to Lenny Bruce's joke on the same theme). He has implied that the name change was due to a strained relationship with his father, with whom Stewart no longer had any contact.

What is Jon Stewart known for?
Jon Stewart is primarily known for his role as the host of "The Daily Show," a satirical news and comedy television program. During his time on the show from 1999 to 2015, he gained fame for his incisive political commentary and humorous take on current events. He also has had many appearances as actor in films such as:
  • Half Baked
  • The Faculty
  • Big Daddy
  • Death to Smoochy
  • Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice

Is Jon Stewart married?
Yes, Jon Stewart is married to Tracey McShane. While making the 1997 film, Wishful Thinking, a production assistant on the film arranged for a blind date between Stewart and Tracey Lynn McShane. They dated for four years. Stewart proposed to her through a personalized crossword puzzle created with the help of Will Shortz, the crossword editor at The New York Times. They married in 2000.

Does Jon Stewart have children?
Jon Stewart and his wife, Tracey McShane, are parents to two children. They have a son named Nathan Thomas Stewart born on July 3, 2004 and a daughter named Maggie Rose Stewart who was born on February 4, 2006. The family lives on a 12-acre (4.9 ha) farm in Middletown, New Jersey, called "Bufflehead Farm". The Stewarts operate it as a sanctuary for abused animals.

Who are Jon Stewart's parents?
His parents are Marian Leibowitz (née Laskin), a teacher and later educational consultant, and Donald Leibowitz (1931-2013), an energy coordinator for the New Jersey Department of the Treasury. Stewart's family members are Ashkenazi Jewish immigrants to America from Poland, Ukraine, and Belarus. One of his grandfathers was born in Manzhouli (now part of China). Stewart's parents divorced when he was eleven years old, and Stewart was largely estranged from his father.

What is Jon Stewart's education?
Jon Stewart attended Lawrence High School in Lawrenceville, New Jersey. Stewart graduated in 1984 from The College of William & Mary where he initially majored in chemistry before switching to psychology. While at William & Mary, Stewart became a member of the Pi Kappa Alpha fraternity, but eventually began disassociating himself from them, leaving the fraternity altogether after six months. He was a three-year starter in 1981, 1982 and 1983 with the Tribe men's soccer team. He had 10 goals and 12 assists on a squad that went 40-15-9 (.695) in his three seasons with the program.

Has Jon Stewart won any awards?
Yes, his numerous accolades include 22 Primetime Emmy Awards, two Grammy Awards, and the Mark Twain Prize for American Humor in 2022.
  • Emmy Awards: Jon Stewart won multiple Primetime Emmy Awards during his tenure as the host of "The Daily Show." The show itself also received numerous Emmy Awards under his leadership.
  • Grammy Award: He won a Grammy Award for Best Comedy Album for his book-on-tape adaptation of "America (The Book): A Citizen's Guide to Democracy Inaction," which he co-wrote with the writers of "The Daily Show."
  • Peabody Award: "The Daily Show with Jon Stewart" received a Peabody Award in recognition of its excellence in television broadcasting, particularly for its coverage of political and social issues.
  • Academy Award: While not directly related to his work on "The Daily Show," Jon Stewart won an Academy Award in 2011 for his directorial debut, "The Daily Show." The film, titled "Rosewater," was based on the memoir by Iranian-Canadian journalist Maziar Bahari.
